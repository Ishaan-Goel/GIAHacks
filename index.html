About Us

The GIA Club Chapter at Fremont High School are the hosts for the GIAHacks event. Our aim is to incorporate school students in order to expose them to the latest technologies and the tech community, thereby sparking their interest in the field. We also want to fight global issues in the world.

FAQs

FAQs

Can I participate online in GIAHacks

GIAHacks, a hybrid hackathon, it is a five day online hackathon and a 24-hour offline hackathon & all teammates must be present offline at the venue.

Will you provide Travel, Food, and Accommodation?

We will provide you with food accommodations for offline hackathon. We suggest bringing sleeping bags to the hackathon.

What do I need bring?

An open mind, Zoom, laptop, charger, snacks, food and anything else you would need to hack!

What if I have never been to a hackathon before?

Perfect! We welcome hackers of all experience levels and backgrounds.

Can I come with a project pre-built or will I have to build from scratch?

You can come with a pre-made project, however making a project during the time given is ideal.

How many people can be on a team?

At 2 - 6 people can be on a team.

Is Admission paid?

No, admission to the event is free!

Who can participate?

Any highschool student can participate in this event. (Ages 14 - 18)

I don't want to follow a track, can I come up with my own idea?

Yes! We encourage out of the box Ideas you can build anything that isn't part of the six tracks, but it needs to be related to global issues.

What if I can't find my teammates?

We require at least 2 number of members in a team, if you can't find a teammate then you can work indivualualy.

Where do I have to submit my project?

  • You have have to submit your project in-person during hackathon.
  • You also have to submit your project idea to an email provided you will get after registration.

More questions?

You can reach us out on our Discord, or you can mail us at goelishaan2007@gmail.com. Happy Hacking!!
